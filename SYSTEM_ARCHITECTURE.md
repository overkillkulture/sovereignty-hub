### Multiplier Effects

Some pillars do not just add value -- they MULTIPLY the value of others.

**Manufacturing (7)** is the biggest multiplier. Every pillar has a "What We Make" section that depends on Manufacturing existing. Without it, you buy retail. With it, you produce at 40-80% margin. Manufacturing turns $1 of filament into $5-20 of finished product across all 12 other pillars.

**Communication (6)** is the second biggest multiplier. Without it, every hub is isolated. With it, water quality data is shared, growing data is shared, STL libraries are shared, mesh coverage extends, emergency alerts work. Communication does not make money directly -- it makes everything else make MORE money.

**Trade (10)** is the third multiplier. Without a marketplace, every pillar sells to walk-in customers only. With Trade, every product in every pillar reaches every customer in the network.

**Knowledge (12)** is the fourth multiplier. Every class taught in one pillar creates a customer who needs products from three other pillars. The person who takes the water security class buys a solar panel (Energy), a gravity filter kit (Water/Manufacturing), and a foraging guide (Food/Knowledge).

### Bottlenecks

**Energy (4)** is the primary bottleneck. Almost everything needs power. In North Idaho winter, solar produces 2-3 kWh/day from a 1kW array. Micro-hydro is the solution (12 kWh/day from the same creek, year-round), but it requires a creek. Without reliable energy, Layers 3-6 of most pillars cannot operate.

**Shelter (3)** is the secondary bottleneck. Every pillar needs a physical space. The workshop, the classroom, the storage room, the marketplace -- all need a building. In North Idaho, that building needs heat. Shelter + Energy together unlock everything else.
