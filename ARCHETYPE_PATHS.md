# CHOOSE YOUR PATH
## 9 Archetypes. 9 Quests. One Civilization.
## "Tell me who you are. I'll tell you what to do."
## Created: April 29, 2026

---

## HOW THIS WORKS

You don't need to do everything. You don't need to understand the whole system. You just need to start where YOU are, with what YOU have, doing what YOU already know how to do.

Each path is 20 steps. The first 5 are things you can do THIS WEEK. The next 5 build on those. By step 20, you're running a small business that makes your community stronger — whether the grid is up or down.

Every path connects to every other path. The grandma teaching canning needs jars from the tradesman. The teenager running the mesh network needs the veteran to mount antennas. The business owner stocking the shelves needs the single mom assembling the kits. Nobody does this alone. That's the point.
